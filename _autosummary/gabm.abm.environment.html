gabm.abm.environment module

Defines the generic Environment class.

class gabm.abm.environment.Environment(year: int = 2026, place: str = 'Earth')

Bases: object

The Environment class represents the shared context in which agents and groups interact. It can be extended to represent specific types of environments (e.g., a Nation). Attributes:

year (int): The current year in the simulation. place (str): The name of the place or environment. agents_active (Dict[int, Agent]): A dictionary of active agents, keyed by their unique id. agents_inactive (Dict[int, Agent]): A dictionary of inactive agents, keyed by their unique id. groups_active (Dict[int, Group]): A dictionary of active groups, keyed by their unique id. groups_inactive (Dict[int, Group]): A dictionary of inactive groups, keyed by their unique id.
